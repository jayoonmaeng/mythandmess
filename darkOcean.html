I've been studying art and its various forms for a long time now, but lately, I'm not quite sure what I'm doing or where I'm headed. I know that all the small moments of practice and learning add up to shape me and improve my skills, but I often feel lost and melancholic. It's been almost 30 years since I started building my knowledge and experience, but lately, I feel like I can't keep up with the rapid changes in the world. Everyone else seems to be ahead of me, and I feel like I'm constantly falling behind, always needing to learn more just to keep up with the times.
